## 2.0 | Introduction どうにゅう

Welcome to the second lesson of the katakana alphabet! In this lesson, we will learn how to write カ (ka), キ (ki), ク (ku), ケ (ke), コ (ko), ガ (ga), ギ (gi), グ (gu), ゲ (ge), and ゴ (go).
